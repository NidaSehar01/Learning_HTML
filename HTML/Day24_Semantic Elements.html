WHO

The World Health Organization (WHO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations responsible for international public health.

WHO's symbol

The emblem consists of the United Nations symbol surmounted by a staff with a snake coiling round it. The staff with the snake has long been a symbol of medicine and the medical profession.
